Emerging leaders · Built for the moments that matter

Leadership is built in moments.

Leadra is a behavioural development app for emerging leaders. It helps you recognise the pressure spikes, difficult conversations, and unexpected changes — and build the capabilities to navigate them with confidence.

Five capabilities. One leadership identity.

Each capability is taught through micro-moments, short practice tasks, and immediate reflection loops — ensuring learning is contextual, frequent, and measurable.

ER

Emotional Regulation

Stay grounded when pressure spikes

CT

Critical Thinking

Cut through noise and make sharper decisions

SJ

Situational Judgement

Read the moment and respond with intention

CA

Change Agility

Stay steady and effective when things shift

IN

Influence

Move people without relying on authority

Grounded in behavioural science.

Leadra translates evidence-based principles from leadership development, organisational behaviour, and coaching into a practical, in-the-moment learning system.

01

Deliberate practice

Learning applied to real workplace tasks, with repeated and spaced practice, produces higher impact and better retention.

02

Behavioural principles

Small, observable behaviours shaped by clear prompts and timely feedback produce measurable changes in how you lead.

03

Personalised feedback

Combining micro-coaching with automated nudges increases self-efficacy and translates insight into real-world action.
